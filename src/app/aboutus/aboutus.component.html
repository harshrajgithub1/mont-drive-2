About Us

Mont Drive is the subsidiary brand name under J.D. Engineering Works. It manufactures Gearless Elevator Traction Machine or Gearless Elevator Motor. Mr. Gurdavinder Singh, Founder & CEO of J.D. Engineering Works is enthusiastic to get the technical advancement to India. He has a combined experience in engineering passed from IITs and he is able to reform motor designing. He has forty years of experience in this field. Constant improvement in the various energy efficiency is his main focus.

A subsidiary of J.D. Engineering Works, Mont Drive has become a leading name in the field of Gearless Elevator Machines, or as also called Gearless Elevator Traction Machines.

Incorporating technical excellence and an international-level design and manufacturing process, the company has grown into stature as a quality and affordable solution provider in this field. The company has a wide spectrum of product line that includes 6 passenger, 8 passenger, 10 passenger and 13 passenger Gearless Elevator Traction Machines. The products are designed and crafted with meticulous planning, and are passed through with strict quality control and testing procedures, making these ideally safe and durable for a wide range of premise applications. We serve the clients globally, exporting our entire product range worldwide.
